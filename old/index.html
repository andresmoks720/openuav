Vision

The mission of Open UAV is to provide educational and research organizations with UAV designs to reasearch UAVs and their applications.

Open UAV project provides fixed-wing UAV plans online for people to contribute to the design process or build, fly and maintain their own unmanned aircraft for research or leisure.

Mission

To achieve this goal, we provide a collaboration palform to design a modular UAV platform
